Villains

  • Loki

    Loki was the villain responsible for the first meeting of the Avengers, group of superheroes formed by his arch-enemy and adopted brother.

  • Ultron

    Ultron was an artificial intelligence peacekeeping program created by Iron Man using decrypted code derived from the Mind Stone enclosed in Loki's Scepter, with the purpose of protecting all humanity, but Ultron considered humanity itself to be Earth's greatest threat.

  • Thanos

    Thanos is from a planet called Titan and due to its super population he proposed as a solution to eliminate half of the people on the planet, having his proposal rejected he was expelled from the planet. Thanos witnessed the death of his people until he was the only survivor of the Titan race. After seeing what happened to Titan, Thanos concluded that other planets would eventually suffer the same fate, and believed it was his destiny to eliminate half of the universe's population so that the survivors could prosper.
